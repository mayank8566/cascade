About CASCADE

Our Origin Story

CASCADE was born from the fusion of two legendary Minecraft teams: RUPTURES and FAITH. What started as an alliance quickly evolved into something greater - a unified force with a shared vision of dominance and excellence in the Minecraft universe.

The Meaning Behind CASCADE

The name CASCADE represents our team's ability to flow seamlessly together, unleashing a cascade of skills and strategies that overwhelm our opponents. Like a cosmic waterfall of stars, we bring beauty and power to every server we join.
